Help & Support

If you're having trouble with the app, here are some common solutions:

1. Update the App: Make sure you're using the latest version of the app. You can check for updates in the App Store.

2. Restart the App: If the app is behaving strangely, try closing it completely and then reopening it.

3. Check Your Internet Connection: The app requires a stable internet connection to function properly. If you're having trouble, try switching to a different Wi-Fi network or turning on mobile data.

4. Log Out and Log In: If you're having trouble with your account, try logging out and then logging back in.

5. Contact Us: If you're still having trouble, you can contact us directly. We're here to help!

For any other queries or issues, please reach out to our support team at support@dogmeetdog.com. We're always here to help you and your furry friend find the perfect match!
